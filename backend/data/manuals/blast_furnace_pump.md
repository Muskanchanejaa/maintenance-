title: Blast Furnace Cooling Pump Manual
section: Low flow and cavitation
---
Low discharge flow with falling suction pressure indicates blocked strainer, suction air ingress, cavitation, or impeller damage. A power-failure-style torque and speed imbalance is treated as a pump load instability warning. Operation below minimum cooling flow can create tuyere overheating risk and must be escalated immediately.

title: SOP BF-09 Cooling Water Pump Alarm
section: Field checks
---
Check suction valve position, strainer differential pressure, casing vent, seal leakage, bearing temperature, and standby pump readiness. If flow remains below the minimum stable band for more than 10 minutes, start standby pump and create urgent work order.
