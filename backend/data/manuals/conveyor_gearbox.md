title: Conveyor Gearbox Maintenance Manual
section: Oil contamination
---
Particle count above the oil cleanliness band requires oil inspection, breather cleaning, gasket check, and ferrography sample. Rising vibration with tool-wear-style telemetry suggests gear tooth wear or bearing wear. Avoid high-load operation until oil condition is restored.

title: SOP CV-04 Gearbox Oil Alert
section: Corrective action
---
Take oil sample, inspect magnetic plug, replace oil if particles exceed limit, clean breather, verify inspection cover seal, and schedule borescope if particles remain elevated after one shift.
